Health & Welfare

Onsite Nurse

We have a nurse onsite, Monday - Friday between 8am and 16pm. The nurse's station is located on the bottom floor opposite the canteen.

Although no appointment is necessary. You can email or call 2064 to arrange an appointment.

Private Medical Insurance

You are automatically enrolled on the AXA PPP Healthcare plan.

If you wish to enrol or add/remove dependants in AXA-PPP you must contact to your HR Representative.

Click here to view the AXA-PPP Membership Handbook.

Chiropody

Twice a month we have a chiropodist one site. Sessions are free and last about 30 minutes.

Sessions can be booked by emailing the health and safety team.

Appointments are allocated on a first come first served basis.

Dental Plan

You will have the option to enrol on Denplan in June of each year. The HR Benefits Team send out correspondence which includes an application form with rates of cover and an updated Denplan Handbook.

If you miss the deadline there is unfortunately not an option to join at a later date.

Stress Awareness Sessions

Stress awareness sessions are run on a regular basis. It is our intention that all employees have an opportunity to attend a stress awareness session. Attendance is scheduled by the health and safety team.

However, if you are feeling stressed you can visit the nurse at any time. Or alternatively call the UK Employee Assistance Programme helpline.

UK Employee Assistance Programme

EAP is a free, confidential service available 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

Sometimes, daily life can seem full of challenges, so it's reassuring to know that you've got somewhere to turn to if you need some additional support.

You can contact the EAP: 0800 072 7072 or check out their website

Life Assurance and Personal Accident Insurance

Group Life Assurance

The Company automatically enrols you into the life assurance plan from the date your employment commences. If you are a full time regular employee on the UK payroll you are covered for a lump sum of 5 times your pensionable salary.

Personal Accident Insurance

The Company provides extra insurance in the event of death or disability as the result of an accident. You are covered 24 hours a day, seven days a week.
