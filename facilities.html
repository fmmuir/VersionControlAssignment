Arbroath Facilities

Learning Lab

The learning lab is located on the 3rd floor of the office building.

There are 4 computers available for employees who want to complete online training courses, access emails or view/print pay statements.

The room is open 24 x 7.

Please keep the room clean and tidy and do not remove any of the equipment, stationary or resources from the room.

Bell

At various times daily, a bell will sound throughout the facility. This indicates the following

Time Meaning
07:58 - 08:00 Start Work
12:15 - 12:45 Lunch
15:58 - 16:00 Finish Work

You may be working a shift out with the above pattern. Whichever shift you work you are expected to be at your work station and ready to start work at you appointed start time.

Canteen Facilities

We provide access to water coolers and free hot drinks from the vending machines. These are located throughout the site

There are also vending machines selling snacks and sweets located in the main canteen areas.

The canteen in the main machine shop is replenished with fresh rolls Monday to Thursday.

Employees are also encouraged to make use of the kitchen areas in canteens where there are microwaves, fridges and kettles.

Fire Alarm

The fire alarm is tested every Thursday @ 11am. Should it sound outwith this period, please make your way to the fire assembly point.

If you have not been told where this is, then please ask your supervisor.

Smoking

The use of cigarettes or electronic cigarettes is not permitted anywhere in the facility except at the four designated smoking shelters located throughout the facility.

If you are not sure where these are, please ask your supervisor.

Parking

In Arbroath we have two large car parks. There is a 10mph speed restriction in place which must be adhered to by all employees and visitors.

Safety is at the forefront of everything we do at Halliburton, therefore we ask that all drivers reverse park into parking bays
